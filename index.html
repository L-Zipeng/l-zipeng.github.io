About me

Hi! I am a Ph.D. student of computational linguistics at the Center for Information and Language Processing of LMU Munich. I am supervised by Dr. Helmut Schmid & Prof. Hinrich Schütze and affiliated with the Schuetze Lab. Prior to my PhD study, I received my M.Sc. in computational linguistics with computer science at LMU Munich and my M.A. in German Language and Finance at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
